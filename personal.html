Cooking
I am passionate about food and cooking, and am always looking to try my hand at new dishes and cuisines. I've picked up recipes from mine and friend's families over the year spanning geography and culture, from Italy to South Korea to India to Thailand. This passion in exploring flavor extends to coffee, tea, wine, and amaro.

Nature
I love getting outside and have enjoyed hiking in the White Mountains, Sierra Nevadas, Rockies, Cascades, and the Alps. I also enjoy camping, cycling, sailing, kayaking, and more.
